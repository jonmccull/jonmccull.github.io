FizzBuzz

  1. Numbers that are a mulitple of 3 will give you "Fizz"

  2. Numbers that are a mulitple of 5 will give you "Buzz"

  3. Numbers that are a mulitple of 3 and 5 will give you "FizzBuzz"

  4. Otherwise it will just print the number as is.
